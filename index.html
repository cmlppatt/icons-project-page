1920s Casino

With the prohibition of alcohol and gambling in the 1920s, thrill seekers entered the underground world of betting, card games, craps, and alcohol.

Icon Set Design

September 2024

The icon set of cards, chips and dice is inspired by the thrill, risk, and sheer intensity of the period. The cohesive set is designed using a simple colour palette reminiscent of the 1920s, the red representing the danger or risk and green the luxury. The design also incorporate the following design principles:

Repetition is used to express the uncertainty caused by the many factors at play during a game, one could ask: What will the second dice land on? What is behind the second card?

Space is used to simplify the overall design by using less lines. Critical for icons since they should be resizable and recognizable from afar.

Movement is used to express the fast pace of the game. In games, movements are associated with some of the most thrilling moments like going all in or revealing a hand at poker.
